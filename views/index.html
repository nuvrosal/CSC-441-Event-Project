Welcome to Our Event Project

Welcome to our San Diego Event Planner! This site was created to help people find fun, local outdoor activities happening around the San Diego area. Whether you are looking for a relaxing farmers market, a beach cleanup, a group hike, an outdoor movie night, or a surf meetup, our goal is to make it easy to see what events are available and choose something that fits your interests.

San Diego has a lot to offer, from the beaches and parks to community events and outdoor spaces. Our event planner gives users a simple way to browse upcoming events, view important details like the date, time, location, and description, and register for events they would like to attend.

This project is designed to be clean, easy to use, and helpful for anyone who wants to stay connected with local activities. We wanted the site to feel welcoming, organized, and useful so visitors can quickly find events and get involved in the community.
